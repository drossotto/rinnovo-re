Virtualize your data, ship the analysis.

Rinnovo Representation Engine turns biological datasets into compact, queryable artifacts. You analyze in a virtual workspace and persist the result as a single .rnb file that others can explore without rebuilding your pipeline.

Single-file artifacts

Capture a workspace and its semantics as a single .rnb artifact. Share or archive without leaking internal pipelines, and reload into a live workspace when you need to continue exploring.

Virtualized analysis

Work with logical objects (cells, genes, genomes, BGCs) rather than ad-hoc files. The engine enforces typed semantics so your queries stay predictable over time.

Self-hosted by default

Deploy the engine alongside your existing infrastructure. Connect locally or remotely; your artifacts and workspaces remain under your control.
